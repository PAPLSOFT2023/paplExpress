Elevator Systems Overview

  • Hydraulic Elevators: Ideal for low-rise buildings, hydraulic elevators offer a smooth ride with reliable performance.
  • Traction Elevators: Suitable for high-rise buildings, traction elevators use ropes and pulleys for efficient vertical movement.

Elevator Systems Overview

  • Dumbwaiters: Designed for transporting small loads between floors, dumbwaiters are commonly used in restaurants, hotels, and libraries to move items like food, books, and supplies.
